About This Exercise

The glute bridge activates your posterior chain — glutes, hamstrings, and core stabilisers — while improving hip mobility and spinal stability. It's the foundation of all posterior chain training and perfect for any level.

💪 Glute Activation 🦵 Hip Extension 🔒 Core Stability 🔁 Posterior Chain 🧘 Spinal Alignment
How To Do It
  1. 1 Lie flat on your back with knees bent and feet hip-width apart, flat on the floor.
  2. 2 Rest your arms by your sides, palms facing down. Engage your core gently.
  3. 3 Press through your heels and drive your hips upward until your body forms a straight diagonal from knees to shoulders.
  4. 4 Squeeze your glutes firmly at the top. Hold for 2 seconds.
  5. 5 Lower your hips back down slowly and with control. That's one rep.
Coaching Cues
👟 "Press through your heels, not your toes."
🧏 "Keep your chin lightly tucked — don't crane your neck."
🍑 "Think hips up, not lower back arched."
💨 "Exhale on the way up, inhale on the way down."
